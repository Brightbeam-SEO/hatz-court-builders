<h2 id="what-is">What Is Thai Massage?</h2>

Often called "lazy person's yoga," Thai massage uses the therapist's hands, elbows, knees, and feet to guide your body through stretches and pressure along energy lines. Sessions are typically performed fully clothed in loose, comfortable attire.
