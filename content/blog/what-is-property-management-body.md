<h2 id="what-is-property-management">What Is Property Management?</h2>

Property management is the professional oversight and day-to-day management of rental properties on behalf of property owners or real estate investors.

A property management company handles the responsibilities involved with operating and maintaining a rental property, helping owners reduce stress, protect their investments, and save time.

For many rental owners in Meridian and the Treasure Valley, property management provides peace of mind knowing someone experienced is handling:

- Tenant communication
- Rent collection
- Property inspections
- Maintenance coordination
- Tenant placement
- Rental marketing
- Lease management
- Ongoing property oversight

Instead of dealing with late-night maintenance calls, difficult tenant situations, or vacancy concerns on your own, a property manager handles those responsibilities for you.
