<h2 id="what-is">What Is Aromatherapy Massage?</h2>

Aromatherapy massage utilizes essential oils extracted from plants—such as lavender, eucalyptus, and chamomile—to enhance the healing benefits of traditional massage. Oils are chosen for their therapeutic properties and applied with gentle, rhythmic strokes that stimulate your senses, promote circulation, and support natural detoxification.
