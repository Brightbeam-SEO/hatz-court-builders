<h2 id="what-is">Hot Stone Massage: What Is It?</h2>

Hot stone massage uses smooth, heated basalt stones placed on specific areas of your body or used as an extension of the therapist's hands. Gentle, consistent heat penetrates deep into muscles, helping release tightness and promote improved blood flow—especially beneficial for chronic muscle tension and stress-related discomfort.

<h2 id="benefits">Benefits of Hot Stone Massage</h2>

- **Improved circulation** — Heated stones boost blood flow to tired muscles.
- **Pain relief** — Alleviates soreness in stiff areas.
- **Stress reduction** — Soothing heat calms the nervous system.
- **Increased flexibility** — Warmed muscles become more supple.
