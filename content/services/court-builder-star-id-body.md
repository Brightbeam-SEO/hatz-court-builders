## Court Building FAQs for Star Property Owners

### Can older courts be resurfaced instead of rebuilt?
Often yes, if the base is still sound and the damage is primarily in the surface layer.

### What surface works best for Idaho weather?
Acrylic, cushioned acrylic, modular, and other synthetic systems can all work when matched to site and usage.

### Do I need drainage planning for a backyard court in Star?
Yes. Drainage is critical for reducing standing water, surface wear, and premature repairs.
