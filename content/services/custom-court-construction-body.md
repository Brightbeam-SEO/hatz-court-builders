## Court Builder

Custom work requires a court builder who can connect design decisions to construction details. Surface choice, drainage, color contrast, logo placement, and equipment all affect the finished result.

Hatz Court Builders works with clients who want more than a basic court install. We help plan projects that balance appearance, playability, budget, and maintenance.

A professional court builder can help answer questions like:

- What court size and layout fit this property?
- Which surface system matches the sport and climate?
- Can custom colors or logos be included?
- Should the court support more than one sport?
- How will fencing, lighting, and access be handled?
- Can the design leave room for future expansion?

Every custom project starts with the site. Slope, drainage, access, and surrounding features shape what is possible.

## Common Problems With Poor Custom Court Construction

Common issues include designs that look good on paper but play poorly, colors that hide lines, logos that interfere with play, weak drainage planning, and surfaces that do not match the intended sports.
