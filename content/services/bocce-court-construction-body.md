## Court Builder

Choosing the right court builder matters for bocce just as much as it does for faster-paced sports courts. The surface, borders, slope, and surrounding space all affect how the court feels in play.

Hatz Court Builders works with homeowners, HOAs, resorts, parks, clubs, and commercial property owners. We help plan bocce courts that fit the property and the way people will actually use the space.

A professional court builder can help answer questions like:

- How much space do I need for a full or scaled bocce court?
- What surface material works best for my climate and maintenance goals?
- How should drainage be handled around the court?
- Can the bocce court sit near a patio, pool, or outdoor living area?
- Should fencing, lighting, or seating be included?
- Can bocce be part of a larger multi-court recreation plan?

Every property is different. Access, grade, landscaping, and intended use all influence the final design and construction process.

### Backyard Bocce Courts

Backyard Bocce Court Construction is a popular choice for homeowners who want a social outdoor feature that does not require the footprint of a full tennis or basketball court.

A backyard bocce court can be designed around available yard space, nearby patios, landscaping, privacy needs, and access for construction. Even a smaller residential court still needs proper grading, borders, and surface planning.

### HOA And Community Bocce Courts

HOAs and apartment communities often add bocce courts as shared amenities. These projects may include seating, walkways, lighting, fencing, and placement near clubhouses or outdoor gathering areas.

Community bocce courts should be durable, easy to maintain, and comfortable for residents of different ages and skill levels.

### Resort And Commercial Bocce Courts

Resorts, clubs, and commercial properties use bocce courts as guest amenities and recreation features. Commercial bocce projects often need stronger planning for traffic, visibility, surrounding hardscape, and long-term surface care.
